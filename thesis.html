Our Thesis on Reshaping Work

In the wake of artificial intelligence, skills that were once irreplaceable suddenly become imperceptibly easy to accomplish.

Any range of task, that once took skill and experience to complete, can now be done in minutes without a concrete way to ensure the quality of work.

In the next decade, the traditional work landscape as we know it will look entirely different.

We are already in the midst of an economic and cultural shift.

That is why the job market must adapt to this new paradigm. We all must now find a way to set ourselves apart from what artificial intelligence can do in seconds. How can we build skills and human intelligence that machines can't replace?

We believe Figwork is not just an innovation, but a necessary medium to reconnect employers and workers in this brave new world.

For businesses, Figwork turns uncertainty in hiring into a scalable, on-demand workforce.
